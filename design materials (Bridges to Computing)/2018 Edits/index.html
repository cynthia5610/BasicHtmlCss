Anime(2012)

  • Magi: The Labyrinth of Magic

    In a world where there are mysterious tall towers named "Dungeon" which mysteriously appeared out of nowhere, Aladdin and Ali Baba must strive together and fight. Our hero Ali Baba is a teenager who dreams of conquering a lot of Dungeons and becoming a very rich person. One day, he comes across with a strange young boy named Aladdin who carries a mystical flute with supernatural powers in it. From then on, their adventure began.

    K

Anime(2013)

  • Kill La Kill

    Ryuuko Maoi, awandering transfer student hunting her father's killer, comes to a school ruled by a tyrannical student council president, Satsuki Kiryuin. She became friends with Mako Mankanshoku. And together, they battle the student council throughtout the story with a lot of plot twists.

    Attack on Titan

    Several hundred years ago, humans were nearly exterminated by Titans. A small percentage of humanity survived by walling themselves in a city protected by extremely high walls, even taller than the biggest Titans. Teenage boy Eren and his foster sister Mikasa witness something horrific as the city walls are destroyed by a Colossal Titan that appears out of thin air. As the smaller Titans flood the city, the two kids watch in horror as their mother is eaten alive. Eren vows that he will murder every single Titan and take revenge for all of mankind.

Anime(2014)

  • Tokyo Ghoul

    Ken Kaneki, a college student who meets a woman named Rize Kamishiro at a coffee shop known as Anteiku. While walking Rize home, Ken is attacked by her. She reveals that she is a ghoul, a human-like creature that hunts and devours human flesh. As she is about to finish him off, she is crushed by a falling platform. Ken is taken to the hospital in critical conditionand was given Rize's organs into Ken. He then must deal with life as a half-human/half-ghoul keeping his identity a secret from other humans.

    Barakamon

    Twenty-three year-old calligrapher Seishū Handa, to the remote Gotō Islands off the western coast of Kyushu. Seishū grew up in the city, and moving to the island was difficult. Seishu's interactions with the people of the island, who drive tractors on public roads and don't enter through his front door when they visit. On top of that, Seishū's house becomes a hangout for the island's children.
